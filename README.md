## About Me:
I am a Front End Developer who has a passion for problem solving and working in highly efficient purposeful teams. My drive comes from seeing projects through to fruition and working in teams that bring out the best in each other. 

Before switching to software development, I worked as a Youth Rock Climbing Head Coach and Outdoor Educator for about 12 years. I have also worked in the photography field as a professional photoshop retoucher. With my background in developing teams and faciliting meaningful experiences, I am eager to continue to expand my technical knowledge as well as use my previous experiences to contribute to amazing teams of individuals.
